Shimpaku Juniper

Age of tree 19 years. Trained since 1988. Shimpaku Juniper is a slow-growing evergreen shrub that typically grows over a period of 10 years. The curving base and the lines of the cascading branch are very pleasing. I grew this tree in a black plastic pot for 4-5 years before it was ready for a bonsai pot. To take good care, watering must be done up to 3 times a day in summer of 7cm trees.

Below are the reference websites me and my team searched for this coursework

Our Website-Homepage

Reference Website-Homepage

The placement of heading and logo in the reference website were taken as reference while building our own website. We topped it off by writing a slogan related to plantation. The navigation bar of our website was also inspired by the spectacular design of the navigation bar in the reference website in the right side. Then we completed our homepage by giving the description of Bonsai Plants and some images of the plants available in our website.

Our Website-Products

Reference Website-Products

The way the plants are displayed in our website is inspired from the plants section of GamalaGhar website. We ended up with 9 plants that are going to be displayed in our website including a functional BuyNow button that Thanks the user if any purchase is made.

Our Website-Learn to Care

Reference Website-Learn to Care

We wanted to an extra webpage to our website and the best possible webpage is providing people with the knowledge of plantation and techniques to take care of Bonsai Plants.The website whose screenshot is displayed is taken as an reference to how to display the information in our webpage and we ended up making some changed in the styling part.

Our Website-ContactUs

Reference Website-ContactUs

Contact Us page was confusing but the reference website helped a lot. The way location, phone and email were displayed with a Send Message option was taken as an reference while building our site.

Our Website-Login

Reference Website-Login

Login page is added as an extra page cause it was present in almost all of the ecommerce websites we visited while doing this coursework. GamalaGhar's login page looked simple and easy to build and we took that as an reference and made a login page in our website.
